Stone Lake Mortgage - Helping Rent-to-Own Clients

Stone Lake Mortgage has worked with many clients in rent-to-own programs, including those with Home Partners of America, to successfully transition from renters to homeowners. While we are not affiliated with Home Partners of America, we understand their process and can help you navigate your home purchase.

Whether you want to buy the home you are renting or explore other home options, we have financing solutions to meet your needs. In some cases, we can even help roll closing costs and other fees into your loan, and the deposit you placed with your rent-to-own program may be used toward your down payment.

Loan Program Highlights:

  • FHA Loan: 580+ credit score with 3.5% down, or 10% down for lower scores.
  • First-Time Home Buyer Program: 620+ credit score with zero down payment.
  • VA Loans: 100% financing available for military veterans.
  • Free credit guidance and pre-approvals.
